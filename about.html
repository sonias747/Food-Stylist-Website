About Cara

Cara is a Dublin based food stylist, recipe developer and home economist with over ten years of training in food television, video production, and recipe development across editorial, web, and commercial industries.

Cara's work has been featured in global campaigns for multinational brands, such as M&S, Just Eat, Eddie Rockets and Tesco. A full list of Cara's clients can be found here. Aditionally Cara's work has appeared in films and TV series including 'Calvary', 'The Guard', 'Sing Street', 'The Young Offenders', 'Derry Girls' and 'The Fall'.

Cara started more than ten years ago as an assistant food photographer, having studied Fine Arts at the Dublin Institute of Technology. Her passion for food led her to becoming a chef and subsequently a food stylist.

These days Cara is kept very busy with styling and writing for some of the country's leading publications, retailers and production companies. What keeps her even busier though, are her children Sophia and James and her husband Paul. They get to eat some beautiful looking (and tasting) food!
